in General

I thought I'd share a quick thought about UML diagrams in general. I'm not a coder—I get lost in the little details and it frustrats me. I'm learning to find a rythym and even joy in going line-by-line, but I infinitely prefer the big picture. This is why I really appreciated learning more about UML diagrams and their own variations and heirarchy: they've got every angle of the big picture covered.



TOPIC

State Diagrams


I've talked about UML diagrams quite a bit at this point, so I'll keep this brief. That said State Diagrams are not brief. At their very heart they are what-if diagrams. They are behavioral UML diagrams that lay out all possible states of an entity for an event. I kept with the UML theme of student registration. One example I addressed in my own state diagram of this what-if aspect was 'what if the class the student is trying to register for isn't availble this semester?'.

HTTP URL Connect


The concept behind HTTP URL Connect is so simple I almost wonder if I dont' actually understand it. It allows the program to connect to a specified website and data. That's it!
